Info

The graphs give values in shares per time period, not hashes. This monitor thus assumes that your diff is fixed. If not, the monitor will still work, but may give you misleading data regarding the effectiveness of your miners. It is also recommended that you assign your miners static IP addresses.

Q = acceptance % of shares.
A = miner uptime % while the proxy is active.
